About Us

Welcome to LuxeStay

At LuxeStay, we are committed to providing a seamless, hassle-free platform for finding and booking premium residential spaces. Our goal is to connect individuals with high-quality residencies that suit their lifestyle and budget.

With a focus on user experience, we make it easy to explore residencies, check availability, and book your stay—all from one platform.

Our Story

We started with a single hotel, **Tanaji Residency**, with a vision to redefine the hospitality experience. Over the years, we have grown into LuxeStay—a platform that connects guests with high-quality residential options across multiple locations.

Built on trust and innovation, LuxeStay aims to offer a blend of comfort, affordability, and premium service. Our journey is fueled by our commitment to transforming the way people discover and book residencies.

Our Mission

To provide a platform where users can easily discover, evaluate, and book residencies that meet their needs while ensuring exceptional customer support and trust.

Our Vision

To redefine the experience of booking residencies by leveraging technology, trust, and transparency, making the process convenient and accessible for everyone.
